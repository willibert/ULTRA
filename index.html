Ultra Rock Paper Scissor

Challenge your friends to a classical game of Rock-Paper-Scissors, the objective is to select a item which defeats your opponent.

Rock breaks scissors -> Rock wins.
Scissors cut paper -> Scissors wins.
Paper covers the rock -> Paper wins.

If both players choose the same item, the game is tied and the players play again.
